Email Newsletter Design

My design is for educational purposes only and I am not affiliated with the brand.

I created a web ad for Deva Curl ringing in a New Year. This was on the easier side because Deva Curl has a set layout for the footer and header. So it was a matter of thinking up a concept to put in the middle.

Basically, I scanned all of the emails I’ve received from Deva Curl, recreated the header and footer then paid attention to font pairings and accent colors. I really would have liked to have added some text beside each bundle, but I think it would have been too busy and hard to read. I added top and bottom confetti accents to create some interest in the white space. It was looking a bit off especially without the bottom confetti area.

I relied heavily on the alignment tools - those are a game changer. I don’t know how I did anything without it.
